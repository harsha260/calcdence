# Calcdence

**Calcdence** (formerly CampX Attendance) is a premium, high-fidelity attendance tracking application for students. It goes beyond simple counting by providing smart insights, bunking advice, and automated reminders based on your actual 2025-2026 classroom timetable.

---

## Key Features

### Smart Dashboard
- **Real-Time Tracking**: Stay updated on your surplus or deficit percentages instantly.
- **Bunk Recommender**: Intelligent suggestions that label classes as **Safe** or **Risky** based on your customizable attendance target.
- **Staying Home Toggle**: Master switch to mute all notifications on days you aren't going to college.

### Advanced Timetable & Calendar
- **Hardcoded Reliability**: Includes your exact 2025 weekly timetable (DAA, CD, DBMS, AI, etc.) to ensure precision.
- **Academic Calendar**: View your specific schedule for any date in the semester.
- **Weekend Holidays**: Automatically recognizes Saturdays and Sundays as off-days.

### Smart Notifications
- **Configurable Lead Time**: Choose exactly how many minutes before class (0–60 mins) you want to be notified.
- **Kolkata Timezone Sync**: Hard-locked to Indian Standard Time (IST) to prevent scheduling errors.
- **Reliable Alarms**: Optimized for Android 12+ with automatic fallback for exact/inexact scheduling.

### Premium UI/UX
- **Smooth Aesthetics**: Modern, left-aligned AppBars and a sleek dark mode.
- **Scrollable Titles**: Never lose track of long subject names with horizontal scrolling headers.
- **Visual Analytics**: Bar charts and trend graphs to visualize your attendance history.

## Development

Calcdence is built with **Flutter** and uses:
- `provider` for state management.
- `flutter_local_notifications` for smart reminders.
- `table_calendar` for the academic overview.
- `shared_preferences` for settings persistence.
